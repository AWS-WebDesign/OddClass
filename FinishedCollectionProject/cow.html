Here is my pride and joy, the cow ornament!

The boldest our collection has to offer, this cow is impeccable. Please admire it because it is good. No, great. His name is Socks. See a song!

Socks is a great cow. He lives in Massachusetts with his family and is a favorite Christmas ornament. Want to know more about socks? His favorite colors are red and green, he is inanimate, he loves to jingle, and he is always happy.
